Healthy Breakfast Sandwich

A quick and easy breakfast with whole grain muffins, egg whites, spinach, and fresh tomato.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup liquid egg whites
  • 2 whole-wheat English muffins, split
  • 1/2 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 2 slices fresh tomato

Directions

  1. Cook egg whites in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until opaque, about 4 minutes.
  2. Toast English muffins. Divide cooked egg whites between 2 muffin bottoms. Top with spinach, 1 tomato slice, and muffin tops.
